Number 3: The Lead instruments

The Drums are the foundations of any song or beat. Without them, a song can feel soulless or empty even if the other instruments excels. Genres such as Drum & Bass (And it's sub-genres), Rock, & Jazz have a heavy emphasize on the Drums, they basicaly need these in a song.
